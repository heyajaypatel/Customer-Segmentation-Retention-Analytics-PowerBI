# Customer Segmentation & Retention Analytics | Power BI

## 📖 Project Simple Explanation
[cite_start]This project is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution that helps companies understand who their customers are and why they stay or leave[cite: 6, 156]. [cite_start]By transforming raw data into a visual dashboard, businesses can identify high-value customers, predict which ones are at risk of leaving (churning), and create targeted marketing strategies to improve overall profitability[cite: 13, 262].

## 🛠️ Project "Cover-Ups" (Technical Workflow)

### 1. Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
* [cite_start]**Error Removal:** Deleted null values and handled duplicates to ensure 100% data accuracy[cite: 47, 51, 171].
* [cite_start]**Formatting:** Standardized date fields and created "Month" and "Year" columns for better time-series tracking[cite: 52, 53].
* [cite_start]**Custom Logic:** Built calculated fields for Sales Amount and initial Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)[cite: 173, 174].

### 2. Strategic Data Modeling
* [cite_start]**Star Schema:** Organized data into Fact and Dimension tables for maximum dashboard performance[cite: 180, 190].
* [cite_start]**Date Table:** Created a dedicated calendar table to enable comparison of retention trends month-over-month[cite: 187, 191].

### 3. Advanced Analytics (DAX)
* [cite_start]**Segmentation:** Categorized customers into "High," "Medium," and "Low" value groups based on their spending power[cite: 197, 198, 199].
* [cite_start]**Retention Metrics:** Developed complex measures for Retention Rate % and Churn Rate % to monitor business health[cite: 59, 60, 211].
